Our Story

Mister V’s Pizza Bar is a modern Italian restaurant founded by Charlie Vaccaro, carrying forward the legacy of his father's Dom’s Pizza & Pasta. It offers pizza, pasta, and classic dishes in a lively, welcoming atmosphere.

The menu balances tradition and innovation, with a focus on fresh creations. The intimate pizza bar provides a unique dining experience, fostering conversation and connection. Guests enjoy natural light, upbeat Italian music, and attentive service, making it a joyful and buzzing place where food and memories are created.
