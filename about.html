Our Story

A family legacy, reimagined for today's dining room.

For over three decades, Dominick Vaccaro's Dom's Pizza & Pasta has been a cornerstone of Westchester dining, built on warm hospitality, community, and food that brings people together.

Our Family Story

That foundation of tradition and community set the stage for the next chapter: Mister V's Pizza Bar.

Founded by Dominick's son, Charlie Vaccaro, Mister V's carries forward the values instilled by his father while introducing a fresh perspective. It's not just a pizzeria. It's a modern Italian restaurant where pizza, pasta, and classic dishes come together in a lively, welcoming atmosphere.

The menu honors familiar favorites while expanding into pastas, entrees, and seasonal creations from the kitchen. Every dish reflects the same balance of tradition and innovation that defines Mister V's.

The Pizza Bar

At the heart of the restaurant is the pizza bar, an intimate four-top inspired by Charlie and his wife Lisa's love of sitting side-by-side at the bar when they first met.

Those meals, filled with conversation and connection, became some of their fondest memories. Mister V's carries that spirit forward, inviting guests to settle in with a glass of wine and watch their pizza come to life just steps away.

It's more than dining. It's sharing in a warm, personal moment where food and memory are created together.

What Guests Feel

Natural light, upbeat music, attentive service, and joyful energy.

From the moment guests walk through the door, they feel the difference. Mister V's Pizza Bar continues a family legacy with a brighter, more contemporary atmosphere while keeping the hospitality that made the original family business so beloved.
